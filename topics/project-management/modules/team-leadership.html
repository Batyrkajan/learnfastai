Module: Team Leadership

This module explores the essential skills and strategies for effective team leadership in project management, focusing on communication, motivation, and decision-making to drive team success.

80/20 Study Guide - Key Concepts

Effective Communication

The ability to clearly and efficiently share information, ideas, and feedback within a team to ensure alignment and collaboration.

The 20% You Need to Know:

  • Use active listening to understand team members' perspectives.
  • Be clear and concise in your messaging.
  • Adapt communication styles to suit different team members.
  • Provide regular updates and feedback.

Why It Matters:

Effective communication reduces misunderstandings, builds trust, and ensures everyone is working toward the same goals.

Simple Takeaway:

Clear communication is the foundation of a high-performing team.

Motivating Your Team

The process of inspiring and encouraging team members to achieve their best performance and stay committed to project goals.

The 20% You Need to Know:

  • Recognize and celebrate individual and team achievements.
  • Set clear and achievable goals.
  • Provide opportunities for growth and development.
  • Foster a positive and inclusive team culture.

Why It Matters:

Motivated teams are more productive, creative, and resilient, leading to better project outcomes.

Simple Takeaway:

A motivated team is a high-performing team.

Decision-Making

The process of evaluating options and choosing the best course of action to achieve project objectives.

The 20% You Need to Know:

  • Gather input from team members to make informed decisions.
  • Weigh the pros and cons of each option.
  • Be decisive and take responsibility for outcomes.
  • Communicate decisions clearly and promptly.

Why It Matters:

Effective decision-making ensures timely progress and keeps the project on track.

Simple Takeaway:

Good decisions drive project success.
